Personal training

The scope of practice for a personal trainer has a primary focus on prevention and involves enhancing components of health and fitness for the general, healthy population or those cleared for exercise.

Group fitness classes

Group fitness classes refer to a type of exercise or physical activity that is conducted in a group setting, typically with a certified instructor leading the class.

Body building

Bodybuilding is the practice of progressive resistance exercise to build, control, and develop one's muscles via hypertrophy. An individual who engages in this activity is referred to as a bodybuilder

Strength training

Strength training, also known as weight training or resistance training, involves the performance of physical exercises that are designed to improve strength and endurance.It is often associated with the lifting of weights.
